Paris Tour Package

Quick Info

  • Duration: 10 Days / 9 Nights
  • Starting Price: ₹85,000 per person
  • Departure City: Delhi / Mumbai
  • Best Time to Visit: April to June, September to November
  • Package Type: Couple / Family / Solo

Package Overview

Paris — the City of Lights — is one of the most iconic travel destinations in the world. Known for its romantic aura, rich culture, world-class fashion, and mouth-watering cuisine, Paris is perfect for those looking to experience art, architecture, and love in one place. This package is specially crafted to showcase the best of Paris — from the Eiffel Tower to the Louvre Museum and the charming streets of Montmartre.

What’s Included

  • 9-night stay in a 3/4-star central Paris hotel
  • Daily buffet breakfast
  • Return airport transfers
  • Eiffel Tower 2nd Level entrance
  • Seine River Cruise
  • Paris city tour (Hop-on-Hop-off or guided)
  • Entry to the Louvre Museum
  • Visa assistance & travel insurance

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrive in Auckland and enjoy Sky Tower views.
  • Day 2: Explore Auckland city highlights and Mission Bay.
  • Day 3: Travel to Rotorua via Waitomo Glowworm Caves.
  • Day 4: Discover Rotorua's geysers and Maori culture.
  • Day 5: Fly to Queenstown and relax by Lake Wakatipu.
  • Day 6: Enjoy thrilling adventures in Queenstown.
  • Day 7: Cruise the majestic Milford Sound.
  • Day 8: Free day for optional tours or relaxation.
  • Day 9: Fly back to Auckland, explore or shop.
  • Day 10: Depart from Auckland with memories.
